Weight Loss

Weight loss can have causes that aren't due to
underlying disease. Examples include dieting,
exercise, malnutrition or lack of access to food.

Body Building

Bodybuilding is the use of progressive resistance
exercise to control and develop one's muscles by
muscle hypertrophy for aesthetic purposes.

Classic Yoga

The term "yoga" in the Western world often
denotes a modern form of Hatha yoga and a
posture-based physical fitness, stress-relief.

Musculation

Weight training is a common type of strength
training for developing the strength, size of
skeletal muscles and maintenance of strength.

Cycling

Cycling, also, when on a two-wheeled bicycle,
called bicycling or biking, is the use of cycles for
transport, recreation, exercise or sport.

Fitness Running

Running is a method of terrestrial locomotion
allowing humans and other animals to move
rapidly on foot.
